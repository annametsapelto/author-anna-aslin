Demons of Aldermere

Demons of Aldermere is a romantic gothic gaslamp fantasy series set in the city of Aldermere which is a gothic tourist attraction - and a secret hub of demonic activity because the Veil connecting and dividing the worlds is thin there. Most demons are not dangerous, but there are some that are less amicable.

Demons of Aldermere series consists of several interconnected standalones, starting with The Incubus Teahouse. Each book is concentrated on a central romance, but the reader meets several familiar characters in the later books.
