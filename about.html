Anna Aslin grew up on a farm in northern Finland in the middle of forests and fields. She fell in love with stories as a small girl, especially those that had magic. She could have listened to stories for hours and she became quite a voracious reader. At the age of nine she discovered that there is a whole section of books dealing with magic, knights, castles, witches and wizards and everything else she loved. Fantasy. That became her literary home.

She started to write her first fantasy story when she was thirteen after getting a very strong idea while skiing in the forest. In the next year she filled nine notebooks with the story which had at first been expanded into a trilogy and eventually turned out to be a lot longer than even that. Though despite rewrites, The Firetree has not been finished. Though perhaps one day...

Currently Anna is working on several romantic fantasy stories ranging from the gothic Demons of Aldermere series to more Medieval epic Relics of Wing And Flame series as well as a number of standalones.
