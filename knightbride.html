The Knight Bride

The Knight Bride is a standalone romantasy book set in a pseudo-Renaissance fantasy kingdom of Remaly.

The crown prince Leonard has a major problem: despite his father being dead for more than two years he has not been able to claim the crown because the law says he has to be married to a woman in order to be crowned. And his fianc√©es have a bad habit of getting themselves killed before the wedding. When he meets the young knight sir Gard, he concocts a plan: he can pretend to be a woman but he can also protect himself from assassins. But sir Gard is not quite what he seems. His name is actually not Victor but Victoria.

The Knight Bride is a humorous, polyamorous and queer romantasy story about secret identities, forbidden relationships and found family (of lovers).
